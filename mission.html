THE MISSION

Humanity's first manned mission to Jupiter

...and you can be a part of it.

Why?
Our greatest advancement was the invention of the tool, manipulation objects to be of use to us. Our second greatest advancement was the invention of the Discovery spacecraft, allowing us to travel further than we ever have before. Just as we did with the invention of the tool, we will use the Discovery to learn more about our world and how we can use its resources.
